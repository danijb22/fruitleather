Happy Fruit Year! Hahahahah


Fruitleather and all her fruity individuals have had an absolutely beautiful,
meaningful, and blast-master-fantasma-gasmic time of a year. We started
this crazy project back in 2021 but our first real show, the moment the
baby got up and out of that fruit basket, was in February of 2022. What a
gorgeous joke! From that moment on we zipped around Chicago playing
with new friends and old ones, learning who we are, and never sounding
the same once. Not a single time! The opportunities we were given were
splendiferous and gratitude-inducing and we’re dreaming big dreams of
more to come this year.

Thank you to all of our friends, family, and friends-not-yet-made who
showed up to our shows and supported us and our zAnY ways. What a
ride it was to dance with you all. We took a big ol’ break to crack our
backses and finally do our taxes but the boys are back in town in a big
way and catch ourselves grinning every time we think of the new stuff.

Below’s a little list of all the funzo things we heard in 2022 that kept our
engines burnin. If you liked any of these moments, or have anything you
think we should check out, please email us! We’re always loving a good
back-n-forth. Also, feel free to check our Soundclown if you dig as we
re-clound whatever we feel like feeling like and post our lil thingos as
well.



Ttfn fruityfriends and leatherbretheren, and have a helluva grand 2023!
